About Us

Stufiyan Agro LLC

Stufiyan Agro LLC is a company that has been working in the field of wholesale procurement and export of agricultural products from Bulgaria since 2013.

We have built a successful agricultural business, established reliable and strong relationships with leading grain producers, large and small farms, and major global buyers of agricultural and agrotechnical products.

"Agriculture is not just a business — it is the foundation of every nation. We are proud to connect Bulgarian fields with the world."

— Yani Kolev, Owner
