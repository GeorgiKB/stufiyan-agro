Bulgarian Non-GMO Corn

Bulgaria is a significant European producer of maize (corn), and Stufiyan Agro sources from Bulgarian farms in the Black Sea and Danubian regions. We supply yellow corn dried to max 14% moisture and cleaned to food and feed grade specifications. Non-GMO status is verified by declaration per EU regulation requirements.

Applications

  • Starch Production: High starch content corn for wet milling and industrial starch extraction.
  • Corn Flour & Grits: Food-grade corn for milling into polenta, cornmeal, and corn flour for the food industry.
  • Bioethanol: Energy crop for ethanol fermentation and biofuel production.
  • Animal Feed: High-energy feedstuff for poultry, pig, and dairy cattle compound feeds.
