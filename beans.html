Bulgarian Beans — White & Coloured Varieties

Bulgaria is a traditional producer of premium quality beans, particularly the renowned Smilyan beans (a PGI variety) and white navy beans for export. Stufiyan Agro sources and processes beans from producing regions in the Rhodopes, Thrace, and the Danubian Plain, offering reliable supply for importers, retail packers, and canneries.

Available Varieties

  • White Navy Beans: Small, oval white beans for baked beans, canned products and general food service.
  • White Kidney Beans (Cannellini): Large white beans for Italian cuisine, soups and salads.
  • Coloured Beans: Mottled, speckled and coloured varieties for retail packs and specialty markets.

Applications

  • Retail Packing: Consumer bags for supermarket dry bean sections.
  • Canning: Food-grade beans for canneries producing ready-to-eat products.
  • Food Service: Catering packs for restaurant chains and food service distributors.
