Why Source from Stufiyan Agro?

Stufiyan Agro is a Bulgarian agricultural trading and processing company with strong roots in the grain, oil seed, and pulse markets. Operating from Suvorovo, Varna Province, we source directly from Bulgarian farms and handle cleaning, grading, and packing in-house. All products comply with EU food law, and we work under ISO 22000 food safety standards. We supply B2B buyers across Europe and the Middle East, with minimum order quantities from 500 kg for retail packs up to full container loads for bulk commodities.

Oil Seeds

Our oil seed range covers flaxseed (brown and yellow), coriander, sunflower seeds, sunflower kernels, mustard, and sorghum. Brown and yellow flaxseed are grown on contracted Bulgarian farmland, cleaned to min 99.9% purity and moisture max 8%, with full traceability per batch. Sunflower kernels are available in Bakery, Confectionery and Chips grades with purity up to 99.98%. Coriander is supplied as whole seeds or splits with essential oil content above 1.1 ml/100g.

Grains

We supply wheat, barley, oats, and corn (maize) sourced directly from Bulgarian producers. Wheat is available in milling and feed grades with protein min 11% and test weight min 75 kg/hl. Malting barley meets min 95% germination for brewery buyers. Oats are supplied whole for further milling or as rolled flakes. All grains are dried and cleaned to specification and available in 25/50 kg bags, big bags, or bulk depending on volume.

Pulses & Legumes

Our pulse range includes lentils, peas, beans, and chickpeas — all food grade, cleaned and sorted. Lentils and peas are available in green and yellow varieties with protein 22–26% and purity min 99%. Chickpeas are offered in 7–9 mm and 9+ mm commercial sizes (Desi and Kabuli types). Beans include white navy beans and coloured varieties for retail packing and food processing. MOQ from 1 × 20ft container.

Retail

Our retail line covers processed and ready-to-cook products: rolled oat flakes in fine and thick-cut, split yellow peas for soups, and dehulled (boned) lentils for quick-cook applications. All retail products are packed in 500 g and 1 kg consumer bags as well as 25 kg bulk bags for food service and private label. MOQ from 500 kg, with custom labelling and packaging options available.

All products comply with EU food law. Contact our sales team to request product specification sheets, certificates of analysis, or bulk samples.
