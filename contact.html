About Stufiyan Agro & How to Order

Company Profile

Stufiyan Agro LLC is a Bulgarian limited liability company (VAT BG204623096) registered at Neofit Bozveli 15, Varna. We supply B2B customers across 30+ countries with sunflower kernels, cold-pressed oils, flaxseed, coriander, and specialty flours. All products are traceable from field to final packaging.

Business Hours

Sales and export are available Monday to Friday, 08:00 – 17:00 EET (UTC+2, UTC+3 summer). We aim to respond to all enquiries within one business day. For urgent matters, WhatsApp is fastest — Viktoria Yordanova at +359 889 297 893. Factory visits welcome by appointment.

Q1

What is the minimum order quantity?

For seeds and kernels (sunflower, flaxseed, coriander) the MOQ is 1 × 40ft container (approximately 20–22 MT). For cold-pressed oils and tahinis the MOQ is 500 kg. Smaller trial quantities may be discussed for established buyers — please mention this in your enquiry.

Q2

Do you provide certificates of analysis and product specifications?

Yes. A current CoA and product specification sheet are available for all products upon request. We also provide phytosanitary certificates, fumigation certificates, and origin declarations as required by your import authority. All documents are issued in English.

Q3

What packaging options are available?

Seeds and flours are packed in 25 kg paper bags, 25 kg PP bags, 50 lb paper bags, or 500–1000 kg big bags. Oils are available in 5 L tins, 25 L drums, 200 L drums, or 1000 L IBC. Tahinis range from 300 g retail jars to 25 kg industrial drums. Custom packaging or private label can be arranged for sufficient volumes.

Q4

Which certifications does Stufiyan Agro hold?

We are certified under FSSC 22000, ISO 22000, EU Organic (for selected product lines), Halal, and Kosher. All certificates are current and available for download upon request. Our facility is also subject to regular third-party audits by Kiwa.

Q5

What are your payment and delivery terms?

We typically trade on L/C (Letter of Credit) or T/T (bank transfer) terms. Standard Incoterms offered include FOB Varna port, CFR, and CIF. DAP delivery within the EU can be arranged. Lead times vary by product and season — typical production-to-shipment is 5–10 business days for in-stock items.

From field to final packaging — quality you can trace. Neofit Bozveli 15, Varna, Bulgaria.
