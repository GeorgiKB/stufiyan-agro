Bulgarian Wheat — Milling & Feed Grade

Stufiyan Agro supplies wheat directly sourced from Bulgarian producers in the Varna region and surrounding provinces. Bulgaria is one of Europe's leading wheat exporters, and our network of contracted farms allows us to offer consistent quality lot after lot. We handle cleaning, grading, and moisture control at our facility in Suvorovo before packing or loading in bulk.

Milling vs Feed Grade

Milling wheat meets flour miller requirements: protein min 11–13%, wet gluten min 23%, falling number min 220 seconds, and test weight min 75 kg/hl. Feed wheat is available for compound feed manufacturers at competitive prices with more flexible specifications on protein and moisture.

Applications

  • Flour Milling: High protein, high gluten content for bread flour and all-purpose flour production.
  • Compound Feed: Feed-grade wheat as an energy source in poultry, pig, and ruminant feed formulations.
  • Starch & Gluten: Wheat for industrial starch extraction and vital wheat gluten production.
  • Export: Available for direct export under EUR.1 movement certificate to EU and third-country buyers.
