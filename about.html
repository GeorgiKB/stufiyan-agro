About Stufiyan Agro

From Bulgarian Fields
to Global Tables

Since 2013, Stufiyan Agro has been sourcing, processing, and exporting premium agricultural commodities — sunflower kernels, cold-pressed oils, flaxseed, coriander, and specialty flours — to buyers across Europe, the Middle East, and North America.

Our Story

Over a Decade of Traceable Quality

Stufiyan Agro began in 2013 as a regional grain trader in Varna Province, Bulgaria — a region with some of the richest agricultural land in the Balkans. Over the following years the company expanded its own processing infrastructure, building a full-spec facility in the Industrial Zone of Suvorovo to handle intake, cleaning, dehulling, grading, and cold-press extraction in-house.

Today we supply food manufacturers, distributors, and private-label buyers across Europe, the Middle East, and North America. Every batch is fully traceable — from the incoming raw lot to the final palletised bag — and backed by internationally recognised certifications including FSSC 22000, EU Organic, Halal, Kosher, and KIWA.

We operate as a direct producer and exporter, with no middlemen between our facility and your receiving dock. That means shorter lead times, tighter specifications, and a single point of accountability.
